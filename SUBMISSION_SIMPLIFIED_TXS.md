# How PLOP Fits the “Simplified Transactions” Track

PLOP turns a complex, multi‑step OTC trade into a **single, guided flow**: connect wallet → sign settlement authorization → submit order → deposit once → settle automatically. This makes private, institutional‑grade trading **feel like a simple consumer transaction**.

## Why this advances crypto utility

- **One deposit, no orderbook complexity:** Users don’t manage multiple contracts or price feeds. They submit a simple order and deposit once to activate it.
- **Automatic settlement with safeguards:** BitGo MPC policies + the auto‑approver remove manual approvals, so settlement “just happens” once matched.
- **Cross‑chain abstraction:** Users interact with a single interface while ENS identity (Sepolia) and settlement (Hoodi) are handled behind the scenes.
- **Clear UX for non‑experts:** The UI translates technical steps (encryption, approvals, policies) into a clean flow with explicit prompts.

## New financial possibilities (beyond TradFi)

- **Private, p2p OTC swaps** without centralized intermediaries.
- **Agent‑driven trading** where automated wallets can execute safely without leaking strategy.
- **Programmable settlement constraints** (whitelists, velocity limits, approvals) that don’t exist in TradFi rails.

In short, PLOP simplifies a traditionally complex OTC workflow into a **single, understandable path**, while enabling privacy and programmable controls that traditional finance can’t offer.
